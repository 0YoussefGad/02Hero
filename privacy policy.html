privacy policy

It acknowledges that users provide and share private information, some of which is personal, while other information is publicly available (collectively referred to as “User Information”).

It is essential to begin by defining what this Policy entails. This Policy is a set of internal guidelines we distribute to our managers, service providers, and contributors to inform them of what we consider acceptable and unacceptable practices regarding the handling of User Information. This Policy focuses on how we manage certain aspects of the storage of User Information in the United States, as well as the fact that you may have—through our website—been connected with parties who may access User Information. This policy does not constitute a legally binding, individual, or any other type of undertaking, statement, or guarantee given to users, government agencies, or any natural or legal persons of any kind or description.

We have the sole right and discretion in Ireland to determine the final outcome of this policy, or whether it will affect our organization. We reserve the right to keep such outcomes confidential or to make them public.

The reason we collect user information is that we provide services through our website to our end users. This information includes, among other things, information that enables us to identify and interact with customers in accordance with applicable laws, such as age information, which allows us to determine our customer's legal capacity. This information is also used to enable users to access our website more quickly, such as usernames and passwords, and email addresses, which are used to create user accounts, especially during the short period when usernames and passwords are obtained. This information allows us to interact with the customer, process payments, and provide various services. Such as email address, physical address, and mobile phone number. Public user information does not include information that we infer, collect, or extract, either ourselves or through the use of third-party service providers. Such information is based on the analysis of data we collect from various sources, such as statistics for product usage. We control the requirements for readiness with respect to the processing of private and personal data of internet users. As a general rule, user information is provided electronically and in close proximity to stocks in the daily work environment in certain situations. We have shared user information with other people or third parties.

We will follow what is generally required in this field to protect user information. Although there is no completely secure method of online publication or electronic storage, we will continue to strive to use the necessary distance to provide data to users and not to compromise our own interests or the privacy of users.

We obtain basic user information in most of our online business structures. As a company, it may always be available to trusted agents of our assets and ordinary shares, including, in this case, orientation, acquisition from others. User information is a key element in the evaluations conducted prior to these projects and a primary factor in the immediate processing of transactions. We will ensure that our registered creators handle user information in the same manner as we do, and that they continue to develop and contribute to the project in the best possible way.

Users will be given the option to change their user data, provided that the control is legitimate and does not involve any fraudulent control or self-deletion requests. We will comply with such requests when they are beneficial and in the user's best interest. This policy was implemented in December 2022 and is accessible to all concerned parties. We reserve the right to modify this policy without notice or consent from any party, so please review it whenever possible.
